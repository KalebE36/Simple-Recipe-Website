Breakfast Recipe Descriptors

  • Egg & Hash Brown Casserole

    An egg & hash brown casserole is a delectable choice for the morning of thanksgiving. It containts breakfast like ingredients with the likes of eggs and hashbrowns, which happen to be two of my favorite breakfast items. I would highly recommend this dish to anyone who wants to excite their guests with good and delicious food.

  • Perfect Fruit Scones

    The fruit scones are a perfect choice for someone who wants to give their guests something with a lighter stomach load and taste rather than something that would be more of a gut load. I personally like the fruit scones for their warm and soft texture which allows for an enjoyable eating experience.

  • Pot Hash

    This pot hash is quite the force to be reckoned with compared to the other breakfast foods as it contains some of the breakfast items that most people would enjoy, such as potatoes, bacon, and eggs. The Pot Hash would be a great choice for someone who wants to contain all of these items into a single dish; which would provide a tasteful experience to all of the guests who are willing to try this food.

  • Breakfast Bombs

    The Breakfast Bomb is the bomb when compared to its breakfast counterparts. This breakfast choice is a biscuit with many enjoyable ingredients including eggs, bacon, and cheese while being cooked to a nice warm temperature. This is a nice introductory food before the days festivities of good eating and large feasts.
